Recipe App

Case Study, Interface Design

This app generates recipes provided by the grocery store. By allowing location based tracking, it'll identify the grocery store nearest the user and generate recipes based on their preferences. The goal of the app is to save time by easily allowing users to purchase the necessary ingredients for the recipe all within one grocery store.

Storyboards

2. Goes to Local Grocery store to buy groceries
3. Is having trouble deciding what to buy and cook for dinner
4. Phone notification vibrates to notify fresh app is available
5. Fresh App confirms with user the current grocery store he's located at
6. After preferences are filled out, the app will recommend user recipes to add them to a grocery list
