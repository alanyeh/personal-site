ABOUT

I'm fascinated with both virtual and physical aspects of design. I graduated in 2012 with a major in Graphic Communications concentrating on Digital Media with a minor in Packaging Design. Since then, I've taken Interactive Design courses at the School of Visual Arts and Stanford Continue Education from some of the leading experts in Design Thinking and User Experience. I learned the importance of empathy, personas, storyboarding and iteration in design.

I currently work with as a UI Developer Contractor, working closely with designers to build new product pages. In the past, I helped develop multiple web pages for a couple of the world's leading companies - Samsung and Apple. Furthermore, I worked on visual design layouts for a executive recruiting start-up, TalentSky.

Right now, I'm obsessed with find solutions to daily problems using digital products, which you can see here. I've been designing out a grocery app which helps shoppers discover grocery store specific recipes, designing a solution to improve parking in major cities, creating a Arduino motion sensor for my bedroom and developing a Pomodoro Timer iPhone App. You can view all my work at here or visit my daily blog.

With my background in Design and Web Development, I want to hone my skills as a designer and improve on web/mobile experiences with a design team. I'm a proponent of using empathy and design thinking for solving complex design challenges. I hope to be that bridge between Designer and Developer.

Other than design, I love rock climbing and solo-backing. Having gone on several solo-backpacking trips across the globe, these experiences has humbled my appreciated for differenct cultures around the world.
